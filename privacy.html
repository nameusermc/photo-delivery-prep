Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2026

Photo Delivery Prep is a browser-based tool designed to process images locally on your device. Your privacy is important to us.

Local Processing

All image processing takes place entirely within your browser. Images are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as names, addresses, or image data.

For paid purchases, Paddle (our payment processor) may collect information such as your email address and payment details. This information is handled entirely by Paddle and is never shared with us directly.

Cookies & Local Storage

This site uses local browser storage to remember whether your purchase has been unlocked.

If you clear your browser data, you may restore access using the email address you used at checkout.

Third-Party Services

Payments are processed by Paddle, who acts as Merchant of Record. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.
