# Privacy Policy **Last updated:** 01/20/2026 This Privacy Policy explains how Photo Delivery Prep (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles your information. ## Overview (Plain English) Photo Delivery Prep is a client-side web tool. All image processing happens entirely in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers. ## Information We Do **Not** Collect We do not: * Upload, store, or process your images on a server * Collect personal information like name, address, or phone number * Use analytics, trackers, or advertising cookies * Track your usage behavior Your images remain on your device at all times. ## Local Processing When you use the App to rename images or remove EXIF metadata: * All processing happens locally in your browser * No files are transmitted over the internet * Closing the page ends the session ## Local Storage The App uses your browser’s `localStorage` only to: * Remember whether you have unlocked unlimited usage This data stays on your device. Clearing browser data will remove this information. ## Payments Payments are handled entirely by **Paddle**, our payment processor and Merchant of Record. We do not: * See your payment details * Store credit card or billing information Paddle may collect personal and payment information as required to process transactions and taxes. Please refer to Paddle’s Privacy Policy for details. ## Third-Party Services The App relies on: * **Paddle** – payment processing * **GitHub Pages** – website hosting No other third-party services receive your data. ## Children’s Privacy The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. ## Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: **[photodeliveryprep@gmail.com](mailto:photodeliveryprep@gmail.com)**
